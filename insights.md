\# Amazon Product Performance Dashboard - Business Insights



\## Project Summary



This project analyzes Amazon product data using Microsoft Excel to understand product performance, customer ratings, pricing strategies, and customer engagement. An interactive dashboard was created using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, and Slicers to convert raw data into meaningful business insights.



\---



\# Dashboard KPIs



| KPI | Value |

|------|-------|

| Total Products | 1,465 |

| Average Rating | 4.1 |

| Average Discount | 48% |

| Total Customer Reviews | 26,766,377 |



\---



\# Key Business Insights



\## 1. Electronics is the Largest Product Category



\- Electronics contains the highest number of products.

\- Computers \& Accessories and Home \& Kitchen closely follow.

\- Categories such as Toys \& Games and Car \& Motorbike contain comparatively fewer products.



\### Business Insight



Amazon's product catalog is heavily concentrated in technology-related categories, indicating stronger customer demand and wider product availability.



\---



\## 2. High Discounts are Common Across Categories



\- The overall average discount is approximately \*\*48%\*\*.

\- Home Improvement products offer the highest average discounts.

\- Office Products and Toys \& Games have the lowest discounts.



\### Business Insight



Large discounts appear to be an important pricing strategy, particularly for categories with higher competition or lower customer demand.



\---



\## 3. Customer Ratings are Consistently High



\- The overall average product rating is \*\*4.1 out of 5\*\*.

\- Most categories maintain ratings between \*\*4.0 and 4.3\*\*.

\- No category has a significantly poor average rating.



\### Business Insight



Customers generally report positive experiences across all product categories, suggesting strong product quality and customer satisfaction.



\---



\## 4. Most Products Offer Medium to High Discounts



The discount distribution shows that:



\- Most products fall within the \*\*50%–69%\*\* discount range.

\- A significant number of products offer \*\*30%–49%\*\* discounts.

\- Only a small percentage of products have discounts below \*\*10%\*\*.



\### Business Insight



Amazon frequently uses promotional pricing, with medium and high discounts being the most common strategy to attract customers.



\---



\## 5. Customer Reviews are Highly Concentrated



A small number of products receive exceptionally high review counts compared to the rest of the catalog.



The most reviewed products are primarily:



\- Smartphone accessories

\- Charging cables

\- Earphones

\- Mobile phones



\### Business Insight



Popular consumer electronics generate significantly higher customer engagement than products in other categories.



\---



\# Overall Findings



\- Electronics dominates the Amazon marketplace.

\- Customer ratings remain consistently strong across all categories.

\- Discounts are widely used as a competitive pricing strategy.

\- Review counts are concentrated among a few highly popular products.

\- Interactive dashboards make it easier to identify category performance and customer purchasing trends.
